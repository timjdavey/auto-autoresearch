- Note verbosity
  - More verbose — **richer detail for reasoning**

- Optimisation mindset
  - "Don't always try to beat your last score, explore and fail lots" — **avoids local maxima, gives space to breathe**
  - "Always try to beat your last score" — **keeps focus and momentum**

- Planning vs opportunism
  - Plan upfront and build a good idea set — **structured search**
  - Don't anchor early, stay opportunistic — **avoids premature commitment**

- Explore/exploit phasing
  - Dedicate an explicit exploration phase at the start, then exploit — **but what's the right split? 20%? 80%?**

- Note length vs breadth
  - Long comprehensive notes — **better understanding of details**
  - Short notes — **wider coverage within the context window**

- Review strategy
  - Read all best options, then review for missing gems — **structured phases of thinking**
  - Ignore prior research entirely — **avoids anchoring bias**

- Failure review
  - Re-run previous failures to check if they were just bad luck — **non-determinism means some "failures" may actually work**

- Search at scale
  - How to deal with massive numbers of trials — **what search strategies work best?**
